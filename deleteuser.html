Account Deletion Request

App Name: Fitness Dude

If you would like to delete your account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to urfitnessdude@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include your registered email address

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon verification, we will permanently delete:

  • Your account information (name, email)
  • Your fitness profile data (age, weight, height, goals)
  • App usage data associated with your account

Retention Policy

Some minimal data may be retained if required for legal or security purposes. All other personal data will be deleted.

Processing Time

We will process your request within 48 hours of receiving it.
